About Me

Hi! I'm Shaun and welcome to my website.

Chances are, you've come here because you're looking for either a band, teacher, or musician and I hope I can help. Over the last 10 years, I've had the privilege of working in many different areas within music including live-performance, tuition, studio & session work, arranging and I've even tried my hand at conducting.

It's been great working with so many people and I've found myself settle into my idyllic band setups, all of which are listed here. I'm hoping there will be something here for you, so please have a good look around. Thank you!
